About INKA LittUp

Raymond Ogero started INKA LittUp in 2020, not because he planned to, but because people in Kisii kept calling him to fix their electrical problems during the COVID lockdown. He answered every call. Today, we serve clients across the country; from homes in Kisii to businesses in Nairobi and beyond — and we're proud to be a trusted electrical partner for Kenyans everywhere.

That one-man mission has grown into a team of trusted experts. We don't just do wiring; we handle everything from the piping in the ground to the final switch on your wall. The foundation of safe electricity.

With over 53+ projects under our belt and more than 6 years of experience, we bridge the gap left by Kenya's Last Mile Connectivity. Bringing power to your doorstep is only half the job; making it work safely inside your home is where we come in.

If it's electrical, we do it. And we do it right.

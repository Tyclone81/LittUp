About INKA LittUp

INKA LittUp is a premier electrical services provider based in Kisii, Kenya. With years of hands-on experience in the construction industry, we've built a reputation for reliability, safety, and excellence.

From the foundation to the final switch, we handle every aspect of electrical work: piping, conduit runs, lighting design, switchgear installation, and complete distribution board setup. We work on residential homes, commercial buildings, and construction sites across Kisii and surrounding areas.
